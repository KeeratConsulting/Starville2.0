Painting & Decorating FAQs

Do you supply paint or can we provide our own?

Either. We can supply premium trade ranges or use yours if you already have it.

How quickly can you start in Kent?

Often within 1–2 weeks; share your dates and we’ll confirm the earliest slot.

Do you paint doors, skirting and radiators?

Yes—add quantities in the optional section and we’ll cost them clearly.

Can you work out of hours?

Yes—evenings and weekends for commercial refreshes or tight timelines.

What preparation do you include?

Masking, filling minor cracks, sanding and spot-priming as standard. Larger making-good can be added.

What brands do you use?

Dulux Trade, Johnstone’s, Tikkurila and others to suit the spec and use.

How do you protect floors and furniture?

We protect with sheeting, plastics and careful masking; we tidy daily.

Do you cover exterior weather-resistant systems?

Yes—masonry/render systems specified for longevity based on exposure.
